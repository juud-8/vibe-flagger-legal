# Vibe-Flagger Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 9, 2024**

This Privacy Policy describes how Vibe-Flagger ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device (NOT Collected by Us)

The following sensitive data is stored **exclusively** on your device using encrypted local storage. This data is **never** transmitted to, accessed by, or stored on any server controlled by the developer:

- Vibe Log Entries (timestamps, vibe types, severity scores, categories)
- Person Names (individuals you choose to track)
- Notes and Comments (your personal observations)
- PIN/Authentication Status (your security credentials)
- App Preferences and Settings

This data remains on your device at all times. We have **no ability** to access, view, or recover this information. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, this information will be permanently deleted.

### 1.2 Non-Personal Data Collected via Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect limited non-personal data:

**a) RevenueCat (In-App Purchase Management)**
- Purchase transaction identifiers
- Subscription status
- Anonymous usage analytics
- Device identifiers for purchase validation
- [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

**b) Google Play Billing**
- Transaction records
- Purchase history for your Google account
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**c) Expo/React Native (App Framework)**
- Anonymous crash reports and diagnostics
- Performance metrics
- Device type and OS version (anonymized)

---

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Local Data**: Used solely by the App on your device to provide functionality
- **Purchase Data**: Used to validate subscriptions and unlock PRO features
- **Analytics Data**: Used to improve app performance and fix bugs

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your personal data
- Share your vibe logs, notes, or tracked person names with anyone
- Access the content of your local encrypted database
- Transmit sensitive personal information to external servers

**We MAY share:**
- Anonymous, aggregated analytics with service providers
- Purchase data with payment processors (Google Play, RevenueCat)

---

## 4. Data Security

- All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- PIN protection prevents unauthorized access to the app
- Data is stored locally using SQLite with encryption
- No cloud sync or backup of personal data

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete entries or uninstall the app
- **Purchase Data**: Retained by payment processors per their policies
- **Analytics**: Retained for up to 90 days in anonymized form

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:
- View all your data within the app
- Archive or delete individual entries
- Export your data (PRO feature)
- Uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
- Request deletion of purchase data via Google Play settings

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
